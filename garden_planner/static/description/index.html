You have a succesfull Instagram account and want to leverage the output? This is an extended module which helps you create content for your audience in no time.

Quickly but seamlessly integrate instagram into your odoo website with Instagram Content Loader

Key Features

Use Instagram Content as Blog Posts

Instagram Content loader loads your Instagram posts and Highlights and allows you to use all the native Blog support within Odoo.

Automatic Sync

Your Posts get updated with the default Odoo scheduler. Your Website is always up to date without any manual work.

* unless you want to select the posts yourself

Use Odoo Snipets

You want to use your Instagram Content on any page? With the default Odoo Snipets you can position them anywhere.

Other Features

Posts and Highlights implemented
Select from all your posts
Use Snipets (Features, Media, Image Gallery, Image Wall, Carousel, Media List)
Sync from multiple Accounts
Use without Instagram Business Account and Access Tokens
Support and easy configuration.

How to configure

Step 1 Download the module and place the module in apps folder.

Step 2 Install this module in your Odoo instance.

Step 3 Open Instagram Content Loader > Configuration and create a new entry.

Step 4 Fill in your Username and Password

Step 5 Click on Get instagram posts / highlights tab and sync your posts.

DONE That's it you are ready to use your instagram posts in Odoo (In the Tab automations you can schedule a Job every night to check for updates).
